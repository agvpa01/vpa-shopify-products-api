# Rum Balls Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups vanilla wafers, crushed
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 2 tablespoons cocoa
- 2 tablespoons corn syrup
- 1/4 cup rum

Instructions:
1. Mix all ingredients together.
